electricpy.hz_to_rad¶

electricpy.hz_to_rad(hertz)¶

Hertz to Radians Converter.

Accepts a frequency in Hertz and calculates the frequency in radians/sec.

\[f_{\text{rad/sec}} = f_{\text{Hz}}\cdot2\cdot\pi\]

Same as radsec.
